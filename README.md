# 🛡 SafeRaipur

### Live City Safety Grid — Raipur, Chhattisgarh

**A real-time crime awareness map that watches the news so you don't have to.**

[**⚡ Live App**](https://saferaipur.vercel.app) · Works on any device · Installable as an app

</div>

---

## The Problem

Crime in a city is invisible until it happens to you. News reports scatter across a dozen outlets in two languages. Police data arrives years late as statistics. And the person walking home at 11 PM has no way to know that three incidents were reported on that exact stretch this month.

**SafeRaipur makes the invisible visible — live.**

## What It Does

🗺 **Live Risk Heatmap** — Incidents from news coverage and community reports, rendered as a heat layer over the city. Dark means calm. Color means concentration. Day and night views, because a street at noon is not the same street at midnight.

📰 **Self-Updating News Radar** — Every 30 minutes, an automated pipeline scans English and Hindi news coverage of Raipur, identifies crime reports, pinpoints the locality, and drops them on the map — with a link to the original article on every pin. No editors, no delays, no manual updates. It runs while everyone sleeps.

⚡ **Real-Time Community Reports** — Witnessed something? Report it anonymously in two taps. It appears on every open map in the city within a second — no refresh, no waiting.

🚨 **Surge Detection** — When multiple incidents cluster in one area within hours, the system detects it automatically and raises a live alert: a pulsing ring on the map, visible to everyone, before the news even writes about it.

🧭 **Safest Route** — Pick where you are and where you're going. The app compares multiple walking routes against the risk map and highlights the safest one — then hands off to Google Maps for turn-by-turn navigation along that corridor.

🚓 **Nearest Police Stations** — Enable location and instantly see the closest stations, real distances, and one-tap routing to any of them.

📞 **Emergency Helplines** — 112, Women's Helpline, Childline and more — one tap to call, built into the app.

🛰 **Satellite View** — Switch between the dark ops map and satellite imagery.

📲 **Installable App (PWA)** — Open it in your browser, add it to your home screen, and it behaves like a native app — instant loading, full screen, one tap away when it matters.

## The Idea Behind It

Two streams of truth feed one map. **Machines read the news** — tirelessly, in two languages, around the clock — and **people report what machines can't see**. The database itself pushes every new event to every open screen in real time, and clusters get flagged automatically. No server to maintain, no team required — the system runs itself.

Every incident on the map is labeled by source. News pins carry a link to the original article. Community reports are marked unverified until reviewed. **A safety map you can't trust is worse than no map — so every claim comes with a receipt.**

## Privacy

No accounts. No tracking. No ads. Reports are anonymous. If you enable location, it is used only on your device — never sent, never stored.

## Honest Limits

This map shows **reported** incidents. Real numbers are always higher — most harassment is never reported. A quiet area is not a guaranteed safe area, and route suggestions are ranked on historical data, not promises. Stay alert regardless.
